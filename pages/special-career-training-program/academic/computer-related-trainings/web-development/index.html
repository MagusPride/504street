Chapter 3: Connection

  1. Open Windows Search Box by pressing windows key at your keyboard
  2. Search and Open ENV or Edit the system environment variables
  3. Click and Open the Environment Variables button
  4. Focus on "User variables for Your PC Name"
  5. Click Path then click Edit...
  6. Click New and paste C:\Windows then press Enter on your keyboard
  7. Click New and paste C:\Windows\system32 then press Enter on your keyboard
  8. Click New and paste C:\Windows\System32\WindowsPowerShell\v1.0\ then press Enter on your keyboard
  9. Click New and paste %USERPROFILE%\AppData\Local\Microsoft\WindowsApps then press Enter on your keyboard
  10. Click New and paste C:\Windows\System32\Wbem then press Enter on your keyboard
  11. Click New and paste C:\Windows\System32\OpenSSH\ then press Enter on your keyboard
  12. Click New and paste Google Chrome Directory (e.g. C:\Program Files (x86)\Google\Chrome\Application) then press Enter on your keyboard
  13. Click New and paste Google Chrome Dev Directory (e.g. C:\Program Files\Google\Chrome Dev\Application) then press Enter on your keyboard
  14. Click New and paste Mozilla Firefox Directory (e.g. C:\Program Files\Mozilla Firefox) then press Enter on your keyboard
  15. Click New and paste Microsoft Edge Directory (e.g. C:\Program Files (x86)\Microsoft\Edge\Application) then press Enter on your keyboard
  16. Click New and paste Notepad++ Directory (e.g. C:\Program Files (x86)\Notepad++\) then press Enter on your keyboard
  17. Click New and paste Sublime Text 3 Directory (e.g. C:\Program Files (x86)\Sublime Text 3\) then press Enter on your keyboard
    • Click New and paste XAMPP Directory (e.g. C:\xampp) then press Enter on your keyboard
    • Click New and paste XAMPP Directory (e.g. C:\xampp\php\) then press Enter on your keyboard
    • Click New and paste XAMPP Directory (e.g. C:\xampp\mysql\bin) then press Enter on your keyboard
  18. Click New and paste Git Bash Directory (e.g. C:\Program Files\Git\git-bash.exe) then press Enter on your keyboard
  19. Click New and paste Node JS Directory (e.g. C:\Program Files\nodejs) then press Enter on your keyboard
  20. Click New and paste NPM Directory (e.g. C:\Users\ASUS\AppData\Roaming\npm) then press Enter on your keyboard
  21. Click New and paste Heroku Directory (e.g. C:\Program Files\heroku\bin) then press Enter on your keyboard
